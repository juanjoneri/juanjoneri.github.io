Why Argentina?

The friendly people, laid-back relaxed environment and lifestyle, warm weather, low cost of living and studying, vibrant nightlife and exquisite natural delights are just a few of the numerous reasons why Argentina is on the top of international students’ list for study abroad locations. On the fence about where to go? Here’s why you should choose Argentina!

See the World

Argentina is the Latin American country with the highest number of international students, and one of the richests cultural diversity.

Education

Artentina is home for over 50 universities, recognized worlwide for their academic excellence.

Take in a New Culture

The culture of Argentina is as varied as the country's geography and is composed of a mix of ethnic groups. Modern Argentine culture has been largely influenced by Italian, Spanish and other European immigration.

Hone Your Language Skills

There are at least 40 spoken languages in Argentina. They include indigenous and immigrant languages, with Spanish being dominant. However you will hear it in a wide variety of over 50 variations!

LMU Study Abroad Programs

The mission of the LMU Study Abroad office is to transform our student participants by providing meaningful, culturally immersive and academically sound experiences abroad. LMU has several excelent programs to study and do community service in Argentina. You can learn more following this link.

Casa de la Mateada

Casa de la Mateada is a different kind of study abroad experience. It offers a rare and exciting opportunity for students to experience praxis, community living, critical analysis and reflection, and spiritual formation in the dynamic city of Córdoba, Argentina.

Summer in Cordoba

This 4-week program introduces students to the ethical issues emerging out of the experiences of marginalized communities in Córdoba, Argentina.

Other Programs in Argentina

The government of Argentina, as well as several of the universities there provide opportunities for exchenge and study aborad. You can speak with the study abroad office at LMU to propose a program that is unofficial.

UCA - Exchange program

El Programa de Intercambio coordinado por la Dirección de Relaciones Internacionales y Cooperación Académica (UCA Internacional) te ofrece la posibilidad de hacer uno o dos semestres de estudios en una universidad del exterior siguiendo matriculado como alumno de la UCA. Ello se efectúa a través de distintos convenios con la universidad extranjera.

  • Learn More

ITBA - Global Engineering Education Exchange

El ITBA tiene convenios bilaterales con más de 40 universidades alrededor del mundo. Estos acuerdos establecen que por cada alumno que recibimos de una institución socia, puede ir un alumno ITBA a esa universidad.
